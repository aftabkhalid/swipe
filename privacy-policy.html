Swipe Solar Panel Cleaning Service Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Swipe Solar Panel Cleaning Service (referred to as "Swipe", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you through our online interfaces owned and controlled by us, including www.swipe.pk (the "Site"). Please read this notice carefully to understand what we do with the personal information that we collect as part of your interaction with our services.

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Personal Information: You may be asked to provide personal information when you use our website, such as your name, address, email address, and telephone number.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, we collect personal data in connection with the purchase. This data includes your payment data, such as your credit card number and other card information, and other account and authentication information, as well as billing, shipping, and contact details. Right now we only collect payment via bank or cash.
  • Device and Usage Information: We may automatically collect information about how you use our services, including through cookies, web beacons, and other technologies. This information may include your IP address, browser types, operating system, pages viewed, and other usage information.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • We use the information that we collect to provide our services to you, to communicate with you about your use of our services, to respond to your inquiries, to fulfill your orders, and for other customer service purposes.
  • We may use the information to personalize the marketing and advertising that you see on our Site and to send you promotional messages about our services.
  • We use the information to help us improve the Site and our services, including by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site and assessing the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns.

How Do We Share Your Information?

  • We may share your information with third-party service providers who process data on our behalf to help us provide our services, such as payment processors, software development, or hosting providers.
  • We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the Site or the public.

Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we might provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@swipe.pk.
